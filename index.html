Biography

Meet Dr. Piya Ratna.

Dr. Piya Ratna is a distinguished global social worker, influential youth leader, and dedicated peace ambassador from Kathmandu, Nepal. With a deep commitment to uplifting communities and bridging cultural divides, he has dedicated his work to global peace, interfaith harmony, youth mobilization, and socio-economic empowerment.


His leadership approach blends grassroots activism with international collaboration. He believes that youth are not only the leaders of tomorrow, but the changemakers of today.
