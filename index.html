Meet Dr. Piya Ratna: A Visionary Leader

Dr. Piya Ratna is a distinguished global social worker, an influential youth leader, and a dedicated peace ambassador hailing from Kathmandu, Nepal. With a profound commitment to uplifting communities and bridging cultural divides, he has dedicated his life to the pursuit of global peace, interfaith harmony, and socio-economic empowerment. His dynamic approach to leadership blends grassroots activism with high-level international diplomacy. Believing firmly that the youth are not just the leaders of tomorrow but the changemakers of today, Dr. Ratna has spearheaded numerous initiatives aimed at youth mobilization and leadership training across the globe.

"To empower the next generation of leaders, champion sustainable peace through intercultural and interfaith dialogue, and drive socio-economic productivity on a global scale."

Leading with Vision at Track Nepal

Non-profit Making Social Association Benevolent to Public

As the President of Track Nepal, Dr. Piya Ratna Maharjan leads a dedicated board of directors in championing community service, international volunteer service, and sustainable development.
