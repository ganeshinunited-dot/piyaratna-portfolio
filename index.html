Meet Dr. Piya Ratna: A Visionary Leader

Dr. Piya Ratna is a distinguished global social worker, an influential youth leader, and a dedicated peace ambassador hailing from Kathmandu, Nepal.

With a profound commitment to uplifting communities and bridging cultural divides, he has dedicated his life to the pursuit of global peace, interfaith harmony, and socio-economic empowerment.
